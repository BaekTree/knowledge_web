Section 29 summary

Main Results

This section summarizes the paper’s core experiments across all routing and composition settings. It exists to answer whether compositional skill routing improves LLM agents in practice, and to set up the evidence for the method’s overall value. The main claim is that decomposing, retrieving, and composing skills produces the strongest and most reliable results.
